Tag-Do

solo project

Tired of having a stupidly long to-do list, me too.

Tag-Do is my custom do to list website that allows you to set both the priority and a unique tag to any entry, allowing you to go and search for and sort for your task by tags!

Currently still in development, and is setup for a single user, I plan to make the system feature a login where unique users can have their own lists.

Check it out

Zombie Ducks Like Bread

Group project

This is a small project I started working on in early 2019. It is on the back-burner currently as Escape retirement has become my main focus but was a great learning expeience that I will continue to add to when I can.

The premise is you are the sole survivor of the zombie apocalypse. Ducks were infected somehow and managed to wipe most of humanity. You play as a man in his pyjamas recoveing from a night out, armed with your loaf of bread you need to sneak around the world distracting the duck zombies and find a cure.

I was helped in major part by my friend Joseph of Standing Still who is a fantastic coder of many projects - I will link to some of his work in the OTHER section

There is currently a very unpolished version of this game being worked on, any progress will be appended to the site.

Check it out

Escape Retirement Game

Group project

This is my first large unity project alongside Joseph of Standing Still.

The premise: You are an ex-adventurer in your senior years, after living a life of action you found yourself stuck in a retirement village. One day your grandchild finds your old grapple gun in the attic and brings it to you as a gift. This is your chance to escape! You're to go on one last adventure even if it kills you.

There is a very unfinished build of this game currently being worked on in our free time, a full development log is coming eventually. along with screenshots for each project.
